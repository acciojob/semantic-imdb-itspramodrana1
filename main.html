Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is given a chance at redemption when offered a final job: planting an idea into someone's mind instead of stealing one.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, alongside Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe, all delivering memorable performances.
